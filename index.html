About

Hey, I'm Andrea

And I’m a big believer in taking risks. I deem that “putting yourself out there” and challenging yourself is the only way to maintain a pulse. I strongly believe that success is when luck meets preparation and there’s no better feeling than when the two become acquainted. Ever since preschool (when my favorite color was orange because everyone else liked blue) I have found that a unique spirit is essential to my character and imperative to my personal identity.

I am Chicago-born, Pittsburgh-raised and a New York native. I strive to intersect passion and purpose, because that is the true formula for happiness. I firmly believe that technology is our entire future which is why I traded in my wealth management suit for a developer hoodie and I aim to mash to two together ( I guess into a sweatsuit). My wit and charm will add a pun to any conversation and my “in the moment” personality makes everything my passion project.

Though my passions are intense, life should consist of a plethora of laughing, because otherwise, what is the point? My superpowers are: A 6 handicap in golf, I make a mean quesadilla, I’ve got a great arsenal of sarcasm and notes/lists are my lifeblood.

TL;DR I’m Andrea, I like to laugh and golf. Check me out on twitter and instagram.
